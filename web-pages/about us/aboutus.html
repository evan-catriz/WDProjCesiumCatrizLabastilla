This website aims to bring more information to various endangered species in the Philippine Islands. It also helps bring light to the challenges they face when it comes to thriving in the ecosystem (internal or external) and also how we are their main hope when it comes to survival. It is essential for these endangered species to be more recognized as they contribute to the diversity of our ecosystem. We hope to spread more awareness about the current situation of some of our native species on the brink of extinction and to help or support organizations that focus on taking care of these endangered species, and that there is more to this issue than just their permanent disappearance.
